Langos

Description

Lángos (Hungarian pronunciation: [ˈlaːŋɡoʃ]) is a typical Hungarian street food.

Today it is a deep fried flatbread, but in the past it was made of the last bits of the bread-dough and baked at the front of the brick or clay oven, to be served hot as the breakfast of the bread-baking day.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Make flat circles out of the dough
  3. Fry it in oil
  4. Top it with sour cream, cheese and garlic
